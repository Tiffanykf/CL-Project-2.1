Resonance

In every like, comment, and shared post, we reach for each other through our screens. But our constant search for connection is only ever in the abstract; we interact with artifacts left behind by other digital visitors.

What if you could feel the presence of someone beyond your screen? Let your cursor drift around obstacles until you feel the magnetic pull of another. When you’re close, a yellow circle will appear exactly halfway between your paths and music will start to play. Wander together and the music will flow, but move slowly — there’s nothing cursory about this connection.

Ready to meet?
